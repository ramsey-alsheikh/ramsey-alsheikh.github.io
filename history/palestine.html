Palestine

This little slice of the Mediterranean coast has given the world such a headache. Why don't we dive into its lore a little bit?


     I present to you my attempt at an objective and unbiased account of the history of Palestine. As a Palestinian-American, the history of this land is of great personal importance to me, and I hope have portrayed the information here in a way that makes it important for you too. Please note that this is an ongoing project and thus I am open to suggestion or critique. Feel free to contact me for anything!
